Drink suggestions
Uses camera to take images or a movie of all the products you have. 
It should also been a website that you feed a video or photos to.
Local AI will take that and convert it into a list for you in app. It should include all beverages and tools (ie muddler, shaker etc). This will have to use item identification. 
From there the app will ask what type of drink you would like and will make suggestions based on what you have. 
It will provide the recipe and any directions.
